Executive Summary

KEY TAKEAWAY
Bar Rotunda delivered a strong revenue Q1, with net sales up +14.2% to $248K on nearly flat cover count (−1.7% YoY). Growth was driven almost entirely by pricing power and check composition: average check rose +13.9% to $32.83 and avg cover value climbed +16.2% to $25.23. Private events revenue grew +24% to $56,205 on 6 bookings (vs. 4 in Q1 2025), adding meaningful incremental spend without cannibalizing the walk-in bar. The quarter's story is simple: Bar Rotunda is selling more per guest without sacrificing volume, but the slight dip in party size and covers suggests the concept is maturing into a higher-ticket destination rather than a high-frequency neighborhood spot.

Key Insights

Q1 2026 is a pricing-and-events story, not a volume story. Keep investing in what's working: events programming, check composition, and the 7pm–10pm dinner peak.
