Executive Summary

KEY TAKEAWAY
Le Supreme had a challenging Q1. Net sales declined −15.3% to $1.16M, covers fell −17.8% to 18,508, and private events revenue dropped −25% to $37,060 (16 events vs. 17). The one bright spot is that average check held steady at $154 and party size only slipped from 2.57 to 2.46, meaning this is fundamentally a traffic problem, not a pricing or composition problem. Guests who came in spent almost as much as they did a year ago — but fewer of them came. Guest satisfaction (real NPS 71.1 vs. 71.8) confirms the in-restaurant experience is not to blame. Focus for Q2 should be on rebuilding reservation demand via marketing, programming, and third-party channel activation.
